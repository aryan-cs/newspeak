# Ethics and Safety Draft

## Copyright

The project cites *Nineteen Eighty-Four* as conceptual motivation but does not
copy substantial text, ship an extracted vocabulary table, or use novel-derived
training data. The novel should be treated as under U.S. copyright through 2044,
entering the public domain no earlier than January 1, 2045 in the United States.

## Harmful Content

Safety benchmarks and harmful-compliance prompts should be handled according to
their licenses and safety norms. The public repository should not publish
unnecessary harmful generations.

## Human Evaluation

Human annotation requires rater instructions, consent/data handling, compensation
policy, harmful-content exposure limits, dialect comprehension checks, and
adjudication procedures.
